# V26.1.0 Enterprise Knowledge Center

## Added
- Plugin-style knowledge loader and manager.
- 10 initial industry knowledge libraries.
- 10 initial country-language localization libraries.
- Enterprise terminology, AI learning, template and output-rule foundations.
- Enterprise Delivery Standard (no fixed st08 dependency).
- Read-only knowledge overview/context APIs and admin reload API.
- Enterprise Knowledge Center workspace UI.

## Compatibility
The V25.1.4 OCR, translation, conversion and delivery workflow remains unchanged. V26.1 only adds the foundation layer for Translation Engine 2.0.
